What is JJ's all about?

We are a family-owned local restaurant, that strives to use the best ingredients and sauce to create the most delicious pizzas for our customers. Many of our pizzas use our special secret sauce made by the founder, Jesse. It has been passed down from generation to generation. Many say this sauce is what bolstered the restaurant into the eyes of the public.
